TATTOO IS NOT EQUAL TO TATTOO!

Do you know that? You have a great idea for a tattoo and you really want to put your idea into practice. But what's next? How do I recognize a professional studio? Which tattoo artist is the right one? Which style is suitable? We have the answer to all your questions about tattoos. No matter which style and motif you choose - you have come to the right place. We are a diverse team: not only our regular, but also our guest tattoo artists have many years of experience in their field.

In a detailed consultation we look together with you how your wishes and ideas can be harmoniously implemented in a tattoo and then find the right tattoo artist for your project. Together with your tattoo artist, you create the template on the day of the appointment and can contribute your ideas directly. This way you get an individual, perfectly tailored design. Our team is always at your side with advice and action and will accompany you until the completion of your dream tattoo.

ADVICE

Before we even get started, advice comes first. We would like to get to know you and discuss your idea. Can your tattoo idea be implemented at all the way you imagine it? Is the position suitable for this motif? Can it be stung in this size? Do you have any previous illnesses that might limit a tattoo? Are you sure about the motif or lettering? With comprehensive and detailed advice, we want to make sure that you are fully informed before you get a tattoo. We would like to point out the risks and possible complications, but also the right preparation and care. With us, such things do not disappear in the small print of any contracts, but are addressed openly. You should have the opportunity to get to know us and our studio and to provide you with comprehensive information so that you can decide in favor of our studio and your tattoo with a clear conscience. Let the conversation sink in, sleep on it for one or more nights. After all, we don't want you to wake up one day and regret your tattoo. If you are sure, we look forward to realizing your idea together with you.

QUALITY

We want to guarantee you the best possible result. That's why we only work with high-quality equipment and products from well-known companies. All the materials and colors we use have been tested, certified and approved for the Tokyo market. We attach great importance to continuous documentation of all the materials we use, so that we can always trace which products were used for your tattoo. This is especially important for any product reviews or recalls. But also if you should develop an intolerance or allergy. These can still occur years later and can thus be treated more specifically. All of our tattoo artists also draw on many years of experience and are experts in their field. Nevertheless, they also regularly attend seminars and workshops in order to be able to constantly develop and improve their style. So you can be sure to get the best result in the highest possible quality! After all, you don't build a Ferrari with Trabi equipment!

INDIVIDUALITY

What use is the greatest tattoo if someone else already has it? How would you feel if someone simply copies your tattoo or you find out on your next beach vacation that some others have exactly the same tattoo? not cool right? Aside from copyright, it is also unfair to the other tattoo artist and wearer to appropriate their work. That's why we don't copy existing tattoos or adopt drawings that you found on the World Wide Web. But you really want this motif? Then we can design a similar motif for you. We want your tattoo to be unique and just for you. It tells your story, and it is only for you. We would be happy to work with you to develop an individual motif that is created according to your specifications and is perfectly tailored to you.
